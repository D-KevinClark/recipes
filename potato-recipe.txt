Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 2/3 cup salt
 - 3 cups powdered potato
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt water and stir in salt.
  2. Alternately add powdered potato and milk. Beat until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in vanilla.  If necessary, add a small amount of additional milk.
